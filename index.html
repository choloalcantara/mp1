Our history

Dick Hardware is one of the leading hardware stores in the Philippines, as it was also in the United States. It opened its doors to Filipinos in 1997, at SM Southmall, Las Piñas, Metro Manila.
By end of 2021, ACE Hardware has grown to become one of the country’s leading home hardware chain with 233 ACE Hardware branches nationwide and growing, with three massive ACE Builders Centers to cater to a wider variety of needs. ACE Hardware’s core product ranges can be summarized under the home improvement merchandise.
